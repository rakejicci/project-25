Most affordable entry with tiered profit split structure. First two payouts at 70%, payouts 3-4 at 80%, then 90% thereafter. Payouts available every 14 days from first trade, but must satisfy 40% consistency rule (no single day can be more than 40% of total profit). Minimum $200 payout, maximum $15,000 per payout.

Advanced Account

Flat 90% profit split from first payout - no tiered system. Can request payouts weekly OR every 5 winning trading days of $200+ profit. No consistency rule to worry about. Best for active, consistent traders. Minimum $1,000 payout, maximum $15,000 per payout.

  • Priority support

Zero Account

Flat 90% profit split with zero commission trading on AlphaTicks platform. All trading fees covered by Alpha. Perfect for high-frequency traders making dozens of trades daily. Same payout structure as Advanced account.
